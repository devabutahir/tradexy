Intraday short-term – profit rate (50-65)%

Features
  • Target Audience: Suitable for beginners and experienced traders alike.
  • Trading Style: Focuses on quick buy/sell decisions within a single day.
  • Profit Potential: Optimized for a profit rate of 50-65%.
  • Risk Management: Uses stop-loss orders to minimize risks.
  • Time Commitment: Requires monitoring market movements for a few hours daily.
Key Benefits
  • High Liquidity: Take advantage of the most active market sessions.
  • Quick Results: Close all trades within the same day.
